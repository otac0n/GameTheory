
Joint Buttons and Light is similar to Multiple Buttons and Lights and Best Buttons and Lights.  However, unlike these other games, buttons are not associated with specific groups of lights.  Instead, each button has effects on all groups.  Button aaa toggles the first light in the first group and the first light in the second group and the first light in the third group.  Button aab toggles the first light in the first group and the first light in the second group and interchanges the value of the first light and the second light in the third group.  And so forth.
